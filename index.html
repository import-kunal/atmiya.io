Naresh Joshi

Founder

Mr. Naresh Joshi wanted to combine his entrepreneurial acumen with his technical prowess and thus led to the start of his firm in 1991. Mr. Joshi is known for his infectious calm demeanour, breaking stereotypes on operational and technical levels and always staying one step ahead. His hunger for perfection and achieving excellence in anything he undertakes makes him stand out in the industry. Beyond his firm’s doors, he takes an intense interest in reading philosophy and invests his time in fitness and good health..

Hemang Joshi

Managing Director

Having completed his Bachelor of Science in Chemistry and supplementing this with eighteen years of work experience in the Kutch - Gujrat, Mr. Hemang Joshi brings a fresh perspective to the firm. After sharpening his skills by working with illustrious clients like Frito Lay, Mr. Joshi wants to contribute to the home firm and aspires to multiply the success ratio. He is a firm believer in the dictum that there is no replacement for hard work and that the right work ethic can take you places. Besides his go-getter attitude, he takes pride in being a cricket enthusiast, a nature lover and an avid traveller.
